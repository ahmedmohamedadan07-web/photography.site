Photography Tips

  1. Use natural lighting whenever possible.
  2. Keep your camera steady.
  3. Focus on your main subject.
  4. Try different angles for unique shots.
  5. Follow the rule of thirds.
  6. Keep the background simple.
  7. Practice every day.
  8. Edit your photos carefully.
